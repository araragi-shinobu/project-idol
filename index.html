About Us

Born from a shared love of Yoasobi's "Idol," PROJECT IDOL brings a unique fusion of J-Rock and anime passion to life on stage.

Drawing inspiration from groups like Ave Mujica, we perform under the concept of a "masquerade" where the focus is solely on the music. Behind our masks, we create an immersive experience that transcends identity, letting the sound speak for itself.

Each performance is a journey through emotion and energy, where the boundary between performer and audience dissolves into pure musical expression.
